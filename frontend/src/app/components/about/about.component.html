The goal of KeyboardBuddy is to help people improve their typing abilities through frequent practice. By having a simple and minimalist place to type, the user is able to focus on their typing without any distractions. KeyboardBuddy attempts to emulate the experience of natural keyboard typing, by unobtrusively presenting the text prompts and displaying typed characters in-place, providing straightforward, real-time feedback on typos, speed, and accuracy. Test yourself in various modes, track your progress and improve your speed.
